Yamaha Mixer Help


Microphone keeps losing connection

Step 1

If possible, try placing the reciever. The higher it is the less obstacles it may have.

Step 2

On the reciever, set the squelch to MEDIUM / HIGH.


  1. Press the SET button on the reciever
  2. Press the UP or DOWN button until you see "SQUELCH" on the screen
  3. Press the SET button again
  4. Press the UP button to increase the squelch level

Step 3

Change the frequency to a more stable one.

  1. Press the SET button on the reciever
  2. Scroll until you find the frequency preset
  3. Switch the preset to a different one, ensure that it does not interfere with a pre-existing frequency and re-pair the microphones.

Microphone keeps clipping

Step 1

If the speaker is yelling into the mic, advise them to not or to pull back the mic when they do so.


Step 2

Lower the Gain on either the microphone or reciever

  1. Press the SET button
  2. Scroll until you find the AF out
  3. Lower it until the clipping stops
